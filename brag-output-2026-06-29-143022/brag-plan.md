# Brag Plan: Itti Alliance Finder

## What is this app?
An AI-powered commercial alliance discovery and scoring system for Ueno Bank (Paraguay) that finds, enriches, analyzes, and recommends potential business partners — all running on local Ollama models.

## The angle
This isn't a SaaS pitch deck. It's a working bank tool that uses on-premise AI to do in minutes what used to take a team weeks. The video should feel like a premium product reveal for something that already works — not a promise, not a beta.

## Hook (first 2-3 seconds)
The Ueno blue logo mark fills the screen, clean and still. One line fades in below:

"Finding the right partners shouldn't take weeks."

No fanfare. Just confidence.

## Key moments (the middle)
- **The discovery moment:** The Discover page — a natural language query appears in the search field ("Restaurantes saludables con buen Instagram en Asunción"), AI-generated query tags populate below it, and a results table slides in showing discovered companies with ratings and status badges.
- **The scoring reveal:** The Dashboard — four KPI cards (Total Empresas, Analizadas, Score Promedio, Pendientes) arrive one by one with satisfying weight, then the Top 10 list appears with colorful score badges showing the 8-criteria analysis results.
- **The decision:** A company detail view — the approve/reject decision flow, showing the full pipeline from discovery to human choice.

## Outro / punchline
"398 companies analyzed. 8 criteria scored. One AI pipeline."

Product name: **Itti Alliance Finder**
Tagline: "Intelligent partnership discovery for Ueno Bank."

## User flow worth showing
Entry (type a natural language query) → Key action (AI generates queries, finds companies, enriches data) → Result (scored companies ranked by affinity, human makes approve/reject decision). This is a real working app with a real happy path — show it.

## Tone
- Preset: polished
- Creative direction: quiet premium product film for an internal banking tool
- Interpretation: Confidence through restraint. Fewer scenes, longer holds. Typography is light-to-medium, mixed case, generous spacing. Transitions are slow crossfades. The product is treated like it already deserves attention — not because it's loud, but because it works.

## Duration: 20 seconds

## Audio direction
- Role: warm bed
- Music: happy-beats-business-moves-vol-12-by-ende-dot-app.mp3
- Music treatment: fade in at 0s, stay under at 0.3 volume, gentle swell at 17s before final logo, fade out over last 2s
- Music cue guidance: Preset available — `cues/happy-beats-business-moves-vol-12-by-ende-dot-app.music-cues.json`. Tempo ~110 BPM. Strong cues at 8.74s, 17.47s, 22.93s. Target: KPI cards reveal near 8.74s strong cue, final tagline near 17.47s.
- Audio-reactive treatment: subtle; use music RMS to make the Ueno blue glow breathe slightly on the hero and score badges
- SFX posture: sparse; motion-matched card reveals, one clean transition accent per scene

- Restraint rule: music must never overpower text readability; SFX must not compete with the music bed

## Storyboard

### Scene 1 — Hero Reveal — 5s
The Ueno blue (#0066FF) fills the screen. The product name "Alliance Finder" appears in large, light-weight mixed case. Below it, the tagline fades in: "Intelligent partnership discovery for Ueno Bank." Clean. Still. No motion except the entrance. The blue background has a subtle, slow-breathing audio-reactive glow tied to the music RMS.
Sequential/interaction: no — single reveal
Audio intent: quiet confidence, music bed establishes tone
Audio-coupled idea: subtle blue glow breathes with music energy
Music: warm intro, low energy, setting the stage
Transition mood: soft → Scene 2

### Scene 2 — Discovery Flow — 7s
The Discover page UI is recreated: a clean white card with the search field. The query "Restaurantes saludables con buen Instagram en Asunción" types in character by character. Below it, three AI-generated query tags pop in one by one (beats at ~0.5s intervals). A results table slides up from below showing 3 companies with names, locations, star ratings, and "Nueva" / "Ya existía" badges. The strongest cue at 8.74s lands on the last query tag reveal.
Sequential/interaction: yes — typed query, sequential tag reveals, table slide-up
Audio intent: building energy, the system is working
Audio-coupled idea: each query tag snaps to a beat; table arrival hits the strong cue
Music: energy building, steady rhythm
Transition mood: soft → Scene 3

### Scene 3 — Score Dashboard — 5s
The Dashboard view: four KPI cards (Total Empresas, Analizadas, Score Promedio, Pendientes) slide in from the bottom one by one, each with a clean card sound. Numbers count up to their final values. Below them, the Top 10 list appears — company names with colorful score badges (green for high, yellow for mid). The score numbers tick up rapidly to their final values, beat-synced.
Sequential/interaction: yes — 4 KPI cards arrive sequentially, score badges count up
Audio intent: satisfying weight, data has arrived
Audio-coupled idea: each KPI card hits a beat; score count-up synced to beat grid
Music: full energy, confident rhythm
Transition mood: soft → Scene 4

### Scene 4 — Outro — 3s
Clean white background. The final claim fades in: "398 companies analyzed. 8 criteria scored. One AI pipeline." Hold for reading. Then the product name "Itti Alliance Finder" appears below, with "Intelligent partnership discovery for Ueno Bank." underneath. Music fades out. Silence on the last beat.
Sequential/interaction: no — text reveal, then logo
Audio intent: resolution, confidence
Audio-coupled idea: final text lands near strong cue at 17.47s
Music: swell, then fade to silence
Transition mood: soft — end
